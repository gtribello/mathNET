Taking powers of a matrix : Exercises

Introduction

We can take powers of square matrices by doing matrix multiplication. We thus have: \begin{equation} \left( \begin{matrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{matrix} \right)^2 = \left( \begin{matrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{matrix} \right) \left( \begin{matrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{matrix} \right) = \left( \begin{matrix} a^2 + bc & ab + bd \\ ca + dc & bc + d^2 \end{matrix} \right) \end{equation}
